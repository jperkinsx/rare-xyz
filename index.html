Timed Auctions

The auction primitive that Ethereum has been missing

Fully onchain auction logic with configurable duration, minimum bids, and automatic anti-snipe extensions. No offchain orderbooks, no relays.

  • Reserve auctions — auction starts when the first bid meets the minimum
  • Scheduled auctions — set a future start time, auction runs for a fixed duration
  • Anti-snipe extension — late bids extend the auction, preventing last-second sniping
  • Onchain settlement — winner and seller finality enforced by the contract
  • Revenue splitting — configure split recipients and ratios per auction

Tokenomics

Protocol fees burn $RARE

Every sale through a registered app generates protocol revenue. That revenue is used to buy and burn $RARE — creating real, usage-driven deflation.

  • No fees for direct/casual usage — only app-mediated sales generate protocol revenue
  • Apps set their own fee in basis points when they register
  • Protocol takes a configurable share (default 20%) of each app's fee
  • Protocol share accumulates and is used for $RARE buyback and burn
  • Burn rate scales directly with marketplace volume — more usage, more deflation
